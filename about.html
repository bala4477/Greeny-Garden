About Greeny Garden

Welcome to Greeny Garden Hotel, where the magic began in the whimsical year of 1958. Nestled among the rolling hills since our inception in 1959, we are more than just a hotel; we are a living, breathing storybook of sustainability, serenity, and surprises.

Our Story

Embark on a journey with us, where each guest becomes a chapter in our enchanting narrative. Conceived by a quirky architect and a nature-loving chef, Greeny Garden Hotel is a playground of imagination where numbers are as magical as the garden itself.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We're not just "green" in name; we're green at heart. Our solar-powered garden lights, numbering [Random Number] in total, lead the way to a sustainable haven. Did you know? Our resident ducks, Daisy and Donald, have been quacking joyfully since 1962, helping keep our garden bug-free in the most adorable way possible.

The Greeny Garden Experience

Enchanted Gardens : Stroll through our gardens, where 1950 fairies are rumored to play hide and seek. Each flowerbed tells a story, and you might just catch a glimpse of our resident garden gnome, Sir Greenalot. Whimsical Suites: Enter a world of whimsy in our uniquely themed suites. From the "Secret Garden" to the "Starry Night," every room is an escape into a different realm of imagination, numbered from 100 to 129. Culinary Wonderland: Our restaurant, "The Green Fork," is not just a dining experience; it's a journey into gastronomic fantasy. Our chef, known as the Culinary Wizard, has been weaving culinary spells since 1972, concocting magical dishes that teleport your taste buds.
