Our Specialities

Sustainable Practices: Green hotels typically implement sustainable practices, such as energy and water conservation, waste reduction, and environmentally friendly building materials.

Landscaping and Gardens: As suggested by the name, a "Greeny Garden Hotel" might prioritize lush greenery and well-maintained gardens to create a natural and tranquil atmosphere.

Renewable Energy: Many eco-friendly hotels incorporate renewable energy sources like solar or wind power to reduce their carbon footprint.

Recycling Programs: The hotel may have robust recycling programs in place for guests and staff, encouraging the responsible disposal of waste.

Local and Organic Cuisine: Green hotels often emphasize serving locally sourced and organic foods in their restaurants to support local farmers and reduce the carbon footprint associated with food transportation.

Water Conservation: Implementation of water-saving technologies and practices, such as low-flow faucets and toilets, can contribute to water conservation efforts.

Educational Initiatives: These hotels may engage in educational initiatives to raise awareness among guests about sustainable living and environmental conservation.

Certifications: Many eco-friendly hotels seek certification from recognized organizations like LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) to showcase their commitment to sustainability.

Green Transportation: Offering shuttle services, bike rentals, or electric vehicle charging stations can be part of the hotel's commitment to reducing the environmental impact of transportation.

Community Engagement: Green hotels might engage with the local community through initiatives like supporting local charities or participating in environmental conservation projects.
